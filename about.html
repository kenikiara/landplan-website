About Landplan.co.ke

Kenya's trusted one stop shop for land sales, architecture, construction and property development — built on transparency, integrity and results.

OUR STORY

Building Better
Tomorrows, Together

Landplan.co.ke started in 2015 with a simple frustration: too many Kenyans were being sold fake title deeds, disputed land and plots with no access roads. We set out to build a company that does the opposite — every parcel we sell is verified, surveyed and backed by a genuine, transferable title deed.

A decade later, we've grown from a small land-brokerage desk in Nairobi into a full property solutions company: we sell land, design homes, build them, and develop entire gated communities from the ground up. Over 5,000 families and investors have trusted us with their land and property journey.

MISSION & VISION

What Drives Us Every Day

Our Mission

To make land ownership and property development safe, transparent and accessible for every Kenyan — from the first-time buyer to the seasoned investor.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted land and property brand — known for genuine deals, quality construction and communities that last generations.

Integrity First

Every plot is verified at the Ministry of Lands before it ever reaches a client. We would rather lose a sale than sell a bad deal.

Client Partnership

We walk with every client from the first site visit to move-in day and beyond — our after-sales team is never more than a call away.
