Project Overview

Meadow Heights Estate sits on 45 acres of gently sloping land just off the Kitengela–Isinya road, roughly 35 minutes from Nairobi's CBD. The concept behind the development is simple: give middle-income families and young professionals a genuinely secure, well-planned neighborhood to call home, without the long commute and inflated prices of the city. The estate is laid out around a central spine road with wide, tarmacked access roads branching off to each cluster of plots, so every resident enjoys easy vehicle access regardless of the season.

Each of the 120 units comes with a ready title deed and is serviced with piped water, electricity and fibre-ready ducting laid underground ahead of construction — meaning owners can build immediately without waiting for utilities to catch up. Buyers can purchase a serviced plot on its own or opt for one of our pre-designed 3-bedroom bungalow packages, built to Landplan's in-house architectural standards and handed over on a turnkey basis.

Meadow Heights is designed for young families, diaspora investors building a retirement or rental home, and professionals who work in Nairobi but want space, fresh air and a real sense of community. Communal green spaces, a children's play area and a members' clubhouse anchor the estate's social life, while round-the-clock security gives parents peace of mind. Phase 1 is fully built out and occupied; Phase 2 construction and plot handovers are underway now.
