Due Diligence & Property Management

Verify before you buy. Manage with confidence after you own.

PROTECT YOUR INVESTMENT

Buy With Certainty, Own Without Worry

Land fraud remains one of the biggest risks facing property buyers in Kenya — fake title deeds, double allocations and disputed boundaries can turn a dream investment into a costly legal battle. Our due diligence team carries out thorough title deed searches at the Ministry of Lands, confirms the chain of ownership, and physically verifies survey beacons on the ground before you commit your money, giving you documented proof that a property is exactly what the seller claims it to be.

Once you own the property, our work doesn't stop there. For landlords and investors who don't have the time or local presence to manage their property day-to-day, we offer ongoing property management — from tenant sourcing and rent collection to maintenance coordination — so your land or building keeps generating value with minimal hassle to you.

OUR PROCESS

How We Verify & Manage

1

Title Deed Search & Verification

Official search at the Ministry of Lands to confirm the title is genuine and unencumbered.

2

Land Survey & Beacon Confirmation

Physical site visit to confirm boundaries and beacons match the survey plan.

3

Legal Documentation

Preparation and review of sale agreements, transfer forms and supporting paperwork.

4

Ongoing Property Management

Tenant sourcing, rent collection and maintenance oversight after you take ownership.
