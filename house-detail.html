About This Home

Set on a quarter-acre plot along a quiet cul-de-sac in Runda, this modern 4-bedroom villa combines contemporary design with the comfort of a well-established Nairobi suburb. The double-height entrance foyer leads into an open-plan living and dining area with large sliding glass doors that open onto a covered patio and manicured garden, ideal for entertaining or simply enjoying Nairobi's mild climate year-round.

All four bedrooms are en-suite, with the master suite occupying its own wing on the upper floor and featuring a walk-in wardrobe, private balcony and a spa-style bathroom finished in imported porcelain tiles. The fitted kitchen comes with granite countertops, built-in oven and hob, and a separate scullery/utility area that keeps the main living spaces clutter-free. A guest wing on the ground floor doubles as a home office or fifth bedroom, giving the household flexibility as needs change.

Runda is one of Nairobi's premier residential addresses, known for its leafy streets, low-density zoning and proximity to the UN complex in Gigiri, top international schools, and the Village Market and Two Rivers malls. The estate benefits from private security patrols in addition to the compound's own CCTV system, and the property sits within easy reach of Karura Forest for weekend walks and cycling.
