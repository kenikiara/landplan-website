Project Development

End-to-end development, from raw land to fully sold, occupied projects.

WHAT WE DO

Turning Land Into Thriving Communities

Project development is where Landplan.co.ke brings every one of our capabilities together. We identify and acquire strategic land, carry out master planning that maximizes value and liveability, and manage construction and infrastructure works through to completion. Our team handles the heavy lifting, feasibility studies, licensing, contractor coordination, and quality control, so investors and buyers can be confident the project will be delivered as promised.

We have delivered gated residential communities, apartment blocks and mixed-use developments across Nairobi's growth corridors, including Kajiado, Kiambu and Machakos counties. Every development is backed by clear title, proper approvals and a sales process that supports buyers from reservation to handover. Explore our live and completed projects to see this in action.

OUR PROCESS

From Land to Sold Units

1

Feasibility & Land Acquisition

Market research, site due diligence and acquisition of strategically located land.

2

Master Planning

Subdivision, infrastructure layout and architectural design for the entire development.

3

Construction & Infrastructure

Roads, utilities, security and building works delivered to a phased schedule.

4

Sales & Handover

Marketing, sales support and a smooth handover of titles and completed units.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

Full-Scale Development Support

  • In-depth market research & feasibility studies
  • Licensing, zoning & regulatory approvals
  • Roads, water, power & security infrastructure
  • Marketing & sales support for buyers/investors
  • Contractor & consultant coordination
  • Phased delivery & progress reporting
  • Clean title transfer at handover
  • Post-handover facility management options
