Property Overview

This 1/8 acre residential plot sits in a rapidly developing pocket of Kitengela, one of Kajiado County's most sought-after satellite towns thanks to its proximity to Nairobi, the SGR terminus, and the Nairobi National Park. The plot is positioned in a quiet, already-settled neighborhood with several completed homes nearby, making it an ideal choice for a family looking to build immediately or an investor looking to hold in an area with strong, consistent appreciation.

The plot is flat and rectangular, measuring the standard 50 by 100 feet, and comes fully fenced and beaconed by a registered surveyor so the boundaries are clear and undisputed. It sits roughly 800 metres off the Kitengela–Namanga highway on a well-maintained all-weather access road, with electricity already connected to the neighborhood and a piped water line running along the access road frontage.

Landplan.co.ke has personally conducted due diligence on this parcel, including a search at the Kajiado Lands Registry to confirm the title is clean, free of any caveats, cautions, or competing claims, and ready for immediate transfer to the buyer upon completion of sale.
